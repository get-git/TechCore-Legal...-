Our Vision

In the midst of widespread  wearable technology adoption, Proxemics was created to meet the need for smart respiratory protection. Today there are a number of barriers preventing respiratory equipment from reaching a higher standard of protection. Proxemics aims to utilize sensor technology to monitor key environmental and bio-data to provide real time insight for the user.
